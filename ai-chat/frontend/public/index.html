AI Receptionist Chat Widget Demo

About Our Service

This is a demo page to showcase the AI Receptionist chat widget. The widget appears in the bottom-right corner and allows visitors to:

  • Ask questions about your business
  • Get answers from your FAQ database
  • Connect with customer support
  • Get intelligent responses powered by AI

How It Works

The chat widget integrates with your existing customer database and FAQ system. When a visitor asks a question, the AI:

  1. Searches your FAQ database for relevant answers
  2. Uses AI to provide contextual responses
  3. Can escalate to human support when needed
  4. Tracks all interactions for analytics

Try the Chat Widget

Click on the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to start a conversation. You can ask questions like:

  • "What are your business hours?"
  • "How can I contact support?"
  • "What services do you offer?"
  • "I need help with my account"
